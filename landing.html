About CareerMata

CareerMata was born out of a frustration with job searching. We built an AI tool to streamline job hunting, improve resumes, and help professionals land their dream roles. This project was built as a portfolio piece and continues to evolve.

Developed as part of a personal initiative to integrate AI into career services.
